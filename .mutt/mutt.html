News from Representative Mark Takano

          

Dear friend,

Millions of Americans rely on the United States Postal Service (USPS) to receive their medications and other essential supplies. Here in California, we rely on the post office to vote by mail and to give more citizens the opportunity to exercise their right to vote.

Under normal circumstances, the USPS funds itself. Serving every corner of our country, even those difficult and rural routes that are not profitable or easy to access. The USPS serves every American to ensure that we are all connected, not just to make a profit.

The USPS projects it will lose $2 billion each month through the COVID-19 (coronavirus) outbreak while postal workers maintain the nationwide service of delivering essential mail and parcels, often doing so at a risk to their own health. We must fund and save this essential public service from privatization.

I believe we should save the United States Postal Service. Do you agree?
